Couponing 101

Gather your supplies. Your supplies needed coupons,scissors,store ads, and a calculator. Grab competitor coupons if you have any. Stack your deals. Find manufacturer coupons and store coupons for the same item. Do your math. Makes sure you add up your deal before you go to the store. Have all your deals organized. Don't sweat it if you make a mistake.
